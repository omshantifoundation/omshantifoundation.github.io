Welcome to Om Shanti Foundation established Since 2023

In the heart of Dehradun valley, Uttarakhand, the Om Shanti Foundation was established by our founder, Shri O. P. Agarwal, driven by a deep desire to make a difference. Inspired by his compassionate vision and guided by the principles of humanity, our five trustees are committed to uplifting those in need.

Our journey started with simple acts of kindness—distributing warm clothes on the chilly streets of Dehradun to those braving the cold without shelter. Seeing their smiles and gratitude, we expanded our efforts.With medical professionals among our trustees, we extended support to provide medical care to the needy, believing healthcare is a right, not a privilege. Recognizing education as a beacon of hope, we support children from economically challenged backgrounds, providing resources to help them dream beyond their circumstances. Their laughter and curiosity inspire us to continue.

Understanding that clean water is a luxury for many, we aim to provide access to safe drinking water, ensuring no one suffers from thirst or waterborne diseases. Each glass of clean water reflects our commitment to health and well-being. In the digital age, we promote cyber awareness to protect our communities from online threats. Through workshops and programs, we empower individuals to navigate the digital world safely and seize its opportunities.

Our journey is deeply rooted in the rich cultural tapestry of India and the timeless beauty of Dehradun. Every step we take is a tribute to the resilience and spirit of our people. From the serene banks of the Ganges to the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas, we draw inspiration from the land that cradles us.

At the Om Shanti Foundation, we are more than just an organization—we are a family bound by the shared vision of a harmonious and prosperous community. We believe that by touching one life at a time, we can ignite a spark of change that will illuminate the path toward a better future for all. We invite you to join us. Together, let's spread hope, smiles, and compassion across our beautiful nation.
